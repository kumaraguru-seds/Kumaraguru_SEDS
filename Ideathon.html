IDEATHON REGISTRATION FORM

To spark innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving among students while encouraging them to think beyond boundaries in the field of space exploration and technology.

Rules and Regulations

  • Team Size: Each team may consist of 3 or 4 members. Interdisciplinary collaboration is strongly encouraged, and teams comprising members from different departments will be highly appreciated.
  • Presentation: Teams are required to deliver a 10-minute pitch comprising a 7-minute presentation followed by a 3-minute Q&A session with the judging panel.
  • Lunch and Refreshments: Refreshments will be provided to all participants. However, participants are requested to bring their own lunch or avail themselves of the campus canteen facilities.
  • Code of Conduct: All participants are expected to strictly adhere to the official event code of conduct. Any form of misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Judging Decision: The decision of the judging panel will be considered final and binding on all participants.
