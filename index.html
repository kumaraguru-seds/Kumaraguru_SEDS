Vision

Kumaraguru SEDS is a student organization which promotes exploration and development of Space. We stalk on this mission by supporting a network of interested people and develop their Leadership and also involving them in Space related projects.

Mission

Students for the Exploration and Development of Space at KCT aims to educate and promote space science and technology among students through various events and engineering projects. We nourish our members through constant encouragements, supporting in their endeavors and also create a platform to reach higher altitudes.

Our Teams

Events And Management Team

The Events and Management team is responsible for planning, organizing, and successfully conducting all the events of the club. It ensures smooth execution by coordinating with different teams.

Work of the team

  • Plans and organizes club events, workshops, and competitions.
  • Handles event logistics, scheduling, and coordination with other teams.
  • Ensures the smooth flow of activities during events.
  • Engages participants and creates an interactive environment.
  • Collaborates with the Media and PRO teams for event promotion and coverage.
Team Members

PR Team

The Public Relations (PRO) team is responsible for creating and managing the communication and outreach content of the club.It Enhances the public image of the club by producing creative, informative, and attractive content.

Work of the team

  • Prepares engaging write ups for posters, events, and activities.
  • Invites guest speakers and coordinates external communications.
  • Manages the club’s LinkedIn presence professionally.
  • Involves in the preparation of daily space facts and monthly space news updates.
  • Prepares and updates monthly astronomy calendars with celestial events.
Team Members

Admin Team

This team takes care of the club’s internal operations and financial matters. They ensure the club runs smoothly and responsibly.

Work of the team

  • Manage club communications, schedules, and records.
  • Keep track of members, meetings, and documentation.
  • Prepare budgets for projects, events, and workshops.
  • Track expenses, income, sponsorships, and donations.
  • Maintain transparency in all financial and administrative matters.

Astronomy Team

The Observational Astronomy Team at Kumaraguru SEDS is a student-led group that brings the cosmos closer to campus. They focus on sky observation, celestial studies, and hands-on astronomy practices like stargazing, astrophotography, and telescope calibration. Their vision is to inspire curiosity and foster innovation in space sciences.

Work of the team

  • Conducts telescope calibration, stargazing sessions, and astrophotography.
  • Organizes interactive sessions, student-led seminars, and astronomy workshops.
  • Explores celestial phenomena and evolution of the universe.
  • Connects observational astronomy with real-world space research.
  • Promotes scientific curiosity and collaborative space projects.
